WHO I AM:

Hello, my name is Ryan. I am a full stack web developer living in Brooklyn, NY. I work with HTML, CSS, Javascript, NodeJS, Express, MongoDB, MySQL, React and Vue. Right now, I am continuing to learn more about those and starting to learn about PostgreSQL and TailwindCSS. When not learning or working on a project, I get to hang out with my wife and dog, Sam and Ted. I started running during the pandemic and ended up running a marathon. Now I am training for a 50K ultra marathon. My other favorite pastimes are drinking coffee and eating tacos and pizza. Growing up near or in Cleveland most of my life I was raised loving the Browns, Cleveland Baseball team and Cavs(we miss you Lebron). One day, maybe I will be able to watch a Mets vs Cleveland Baseball team world series.
